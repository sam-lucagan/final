Mission

Our mission is to provide easy transaction and destination menu. Easy reservation for your chosen destination, stress free and hassle free all you have to do is arrive and feel free to love the nature and beauty of La Union Tourist Destinations.


Vision

To create and promote a better, meaningful life for the 'Tara sa Elyu' online community, thereby giving a positive influence for the entire country especially here in the Philippines.


Our Goal

While we are still under the global crisis of corona virus “Tara sa Elyu” will help and guide you for reservations to one of the tourist spots/destinations in La Union. With regards to this, we want to assure the safety of everyone and need to follow the proper health protocol such as wearing face mask, face shield if necessary, own sanitary stuffs especially alcohol, etc., and if possible you are already fully vaccinated therefore everyone can enjoy the tourist spot without hesitation. The number of tourists can be increased by conserving cultural and religious sites, launching cleanliness programs, minimizing internal conflict and providing facilities and security to them. Tara sa Elyu is not just an idea, but a dream that we want to put into realization. That is why we came up with an online local community.
